What is RUSH about?

"Rush" is a captivating short film that delves into the fast-paced world of time management and the relentless pursuit of making it to class on time. This compelling narrative not only explores the challenges of our daily rush but also serves as a source of inspiration that drove us to create this film.

Why we made RUSH

"Rush" is more than just a film; it's a reflection of our shared experiences and a motivation to better manage our time in this hectic world. It reminds us that in the race of life, we should cherish every moment, for it's the journey that truly defines us, not just the destination. "Rush" inspires us to savor the present and make every second count.

Why watch RUSH?

College life is more than just a series of classes and exams; it's a profound chapter in our journey of self-discovery and growth, and effective time management is the compass that guides us through its exhilarating and transformative terrain. This film just acts as reminder of that.
